Common phrases:

- In my (honest) opinion, ...

- I (really) think that ...

- I (strongly) believe that ...

- I (truly) feel that ...

- I'm sure that ...

Semi-formal to formal phrases:

- It seems to me that ...

- I'm fairly confident that ...

- From my point of view ...

- From my perspective ...

- Without a doubt, ...

- I'm entirely / quite convinced ..
