## Books

If you are looking for a book to read in your spare time, you should look for
books at a suitable (= _geeignetem_) level. If you are at the _Gymnasium_ in
Bavaria, look for the following levels: 

- year 5-6: A1
- year 7-8: A2
- year 9-10: B1
